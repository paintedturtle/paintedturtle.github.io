Lefty Tremolo Arm with White Tip

Manufactured by Cort
Product #0074655000
Left handed tremolo arm for a synchronized tremolo bridge with 6mm threads. This part is also known as a vibrato arm or whammy bar.

Chrome arm with white tip. Also available with parchment tip.

The 6mm threads on this arm fit most imported electric guitars with synchronized tremolo bridges made by the likes of Cort, Ibanez, Samick, Squier, Yamaha, etc. Feel free to bring your guitar with you when you drop by if you are uncertain about the size you need.

This arm will not fit Fender or Squier guitars that were manufactured in Japan, Mexico or the U.S.A. The bridges on guitars from Japan and Mexico accept an arm with 5mm threads. U.S.A. bridges accept an arm with 10—24 inch threads.
