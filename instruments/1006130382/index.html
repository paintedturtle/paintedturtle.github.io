Epiphone G-310 (LH)

Manufactured in June 2010
Serial #1006130382

Composition

Solid body in gloss black with bolt-on neck.

Three-ply black-white-black pickguard.

Dark rosewood fingerboard.

Black speed knobs.

Chrome hardware.

Condition

This guitar is all original and it plays like a brand new Epiphone.

Very little visible fret wear.

Typical scratches on the pickguard.

All electronics are in excellent condition.

All the hardware is free of corrosion and blemishes.

The tuning machines for the D-string and G-string are a tad stiff. The rest of the machines are consistently smooth.

There are light scratches on the front and back of the body that are visible in bright light. Not an issue if you play only by moonlight.

Some of the fret ends are a bit sharp; it appears that this guitar has had no fret work done since it left the Epiphone factory. It would benefit from some spot-levelling to normalize a few high frets, and a fret dressing to smooth out the sharp fret edges. We recommend Brian Dubbeldam for this kind of work. His workshop is a few blocks away from us and we can make arrangements with him if needed.

Specifications

650R humbucker at the neck & 700T humbucker at the bridge.

LockTone Tune-O-Matic bridge with stopbar.

Sealed, die-cast tuning machines.

12 inch fingerboard radius.

22 medium jumbo frets.

24.75 inch scale.
