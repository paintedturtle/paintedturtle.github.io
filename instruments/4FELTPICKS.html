Bouquet of Four Felt Picks

Red, Orange, Yellow & Turtle Green
Assembled in Ottawa
Felt picks are great for softly strumming the autoharp, ukulele and every-stringed-thing inbetween. We also like the plunky sound they produce with electric bass guitar.

Each of these bouquets include four picks in four funky colours: Red, Orange, Yellow and our exclusive Turtle Green.

These felt picks were imported from China. We arrange and bundle them into bouquets using pretty things we find in the trash.
