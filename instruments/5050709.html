Yorkville AM50

Manufactured in May 2005
Serial #5050709
Specially designed to accurately reproduce open, airy acoustic guitar tones, this compact, lightweight combo sounds much larger than its size would indicate. Excellent working and aesthetic condition.

Channel 1 accepts a 1/4 inch cable and has a Pre-Amp Gain control, two EQ controls (low and high) and a notch filter to help reduce unwanted feedback with amplified acoustic guitars.

Channel 2 accepts a 1/4inch TRS cable or an XLR cable and only has a Pre-Amp Gain control.

Spring reverb is applied to both channels.

8 inch woofer and Leson tweeter in a sealed cabinet.

Clip indicator lights on both inputs.

1/4 inch line output on front control panel

50 watts output power

12 inches wide, 17 inches tall, 14 inches deep. 23 pounds.
