Aria AB-30 Bass Amp

Probably made in 1987
Serial #87080276
A snappy, almost vintage, 30-watt bass amplifier in good working order.

Two ¼-inch inputs on the front for your electric bass guitar. Input #1 is designed to work nicely with basses that have passive-pickups and input #2 has a -20dB pad to accomodate basses with active-pickups.

Two outputs on the back: a ¼-inch stereo headphone output and a ¼-inch mono line out.

Blue tipped controls for Pre-Amp Volume, Master Volume and three-band EQ (Treble, Middle & Bass). Excellent knob feel!

8-inch Aria speaker in a closed-back sealed cabinet.

This amplifier was (accidently?) sprayed with some dark blue paint on the top of the cabinet by a previous owner. It is not terribly noticeable and it kind of compliments the blue knobs. You can see the paint most clearly in the second and third photos.

The Aria AB–30 was designed for electric bass guitars but it would also be a good small amplifier for electric pianos, synthesizers and quick-n-dirty-garage-band vocals.
