Lefty 500K Reverse Audio Taper Potentiometer

Manufactured by CTS
Product #EP0086L
These are very rare, left handed, 500K audio taper potentiometers. Also known as reverse audio taper potentiometers.

If you need to replace a volume or tone potentiometer in a left handed guitar with humbucking pickups then this is probably what you need.

When installed as a volume control: turn counter-clockwise to increase the volume; turn clockwise to decrese the volume.

24 spline split knurled shaft.

Threaded bushing height of 1/4 inch.

Knurled top is 5/16 inch tall.

Includes nut, lock and dress washer.

These are the highest quality left handed potentiometers that we can find. An excellent replacement for any potentiometer in a Les Paul, SG, Explorer, Flying V and similar guitars.
