250K Reverse Audio Taper Potentiometer

Fine knurled split shaft with 1/4" tall bushing
CTS EP4385L
These are 250K reverse audio taper potentiometers manufactured by CTS for left handed electric guitar circuits. If you need to replace a volume or tone control in a lefty guitar with single-coil pickups then this is probably what you need.

Sized to fit a 3/8" diameter mounting hole on a plastic pickguard or metal control plate. Each one comes with a set of mounting hardware that includes a flat washer, lock washer and a hex mounting nut.

These are the highest quality left handed potentiometers that we can locate. They are an excellent replacement part for most left handed guitars with single-coil pickups.
