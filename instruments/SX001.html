SX Vintage Series P-Bass

Approximately 5 years old
No serial number

Composition

Solid body in gloss black.

Rosewood fingerboard on tinted maple bolt-on neck (with skunk stripe).

Two-ply dark red tortoise shell pickguard.

Chrome hardware and knobs.

Half-round strings with winderfull pink thread.

Small band-aid to cover scratch.

Condition

This fine bass appears to be all original and it is a solid player.

Sadly, it has suffered a 3 inch scratch on the front of the body above the pickguard. In most lighting the scratch is imperceptible, but under direct bright light it looks kinda lame. So lame that we decided to cover it with a band-aid for now. And also because we kind of like the band-aid look.

The tone potentiometer is wearing out and we have a replacement on order that will be installed as part of this sale. All the other electronic components are in good condition.

This bass has been played over the years and there is a some of fret wear at popular positions on the neck. Nothing that affects playability now, but something to keep in mind if you are comparing this to a brand new bass.

There is some buckle rash on the back of the body — light scratches and tiny dents.

Slight scuff on bottom edge of the body.

Pickguard is in great condition and it still has the protective plastic coating on it! It is going to feel good and look even better when you peel it off.

All the tuning machines work well.

All the chrome hardware is free of corrosion and blemishes.

Strings are clean and they have lots of life in them.

Specifications

Pair of single coil pickups wired in tandem like a typical Precision Bass.

One volume control and one tone control.

Open back clover-leaf tuning machines.

12 inch fingerboard radius.

20 medium jumbo frets.

34 inch scale.

Sturdy hard shell case that isn’t too heavy.

Allen key to adjust string saddle height.

Allen key to adjust the truss rod.

Tiny Phillips screwdriver to adjust intonation.

Stubby Phillips screwdriver to turn neck plate, pickup and pickguard screws.

Extra large tortoise shell pick from previous owner.

Three replacement Life brand band-aids.
